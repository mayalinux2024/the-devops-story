# 📖 Chapter 7 — Opening the Lunchbox

The Story

The wizard handed a worker the master lunchbox.

The worker opened it.

Inside were the tools.

The paint.

The robot parts.

The instructions.

Everything was ready.

The worker didn't need to prepare anything.

He simply opened the lunchbox...

and started building.

Each one worked independently.
