📖 Chapter 6 — The Master Lunchbox
The Story

The wizard looked at the blueprint.

Then he built one perfect lunchbox.

Inside it were:

the tools
the robot parts
the paint
the instructions

Everything.

The king smiled.

"We'll never build lunchboxes from scratch again."

Instead...

Whenever a worker needed one,

the kingdom simply copied the master lunchbox.

Every lunchbox was identical.

Nobody forgot the paint.

Nobody forgot the tools.

Nobody forgot the instructions.

The master lunchbox became the standard for the whole kingdom.
