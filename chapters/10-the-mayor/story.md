# 📖 Chapter 10 — The Mayor

The Story

The kingdom kept growing.

There were now hundreds of workshops.

Each workshop had many workers.

Every morning, children placed thousands of toy orders.

Nobody knew who should build what.

Some workshops had too much work.

Others sat empty.

The king realized...

"I don't need another worker."

"I need someone to organize everyone."

So he hired a mayor.

The mayor never built a single toy.

Instead...

he walked from workshop to workshop.

When one workshop became busy,

he sent new work to another.

When a workshop became empty,

he gave it more jobs.

When a workshop closed,

he immediately sent workers somewhere else.

The kingdom became organized.

The mayor didn't build toys.

He simply made sure the right workers were always doing the right jobs.
