# 📖 Chapter 5 — The Lunchbox Blueprint

The Story

The wizard's magic lunchbox became very popular.

Every worker wanted one.

Soon the king asked the wizard,

"Can you make one thousand lunchboxes?"

The wizard smiled.

"I don't have to."

Instead, he opened a large book.

Inside were the instructions for building the lunchbox.

Magic Lunchbox Blueprint

1. Put tools inside.
2. Add paint.
3. Add robot parts.
4. Close the box.

The wizard said,

"I don't copy lunchboxes."

"I copy the recipe."

Whenever a new lunchbox was needed,

the kingdom simply followed the blueprint and built another identical lunchbox.
The blueprint never changed.

Every lunchbox built from it was exactly the same.
