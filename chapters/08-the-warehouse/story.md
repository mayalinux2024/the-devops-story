# 📖 Chapter 8 — The Warehouse

The Story

Soon the kingdom became very busy.

Workers in every workshop needed magic lunchboxes.

At first, the wizard built every lunchbox himself.

But soon workers came from everywhere.

"Can I have a robot lunchbox?"

"I need a teddy bear lunchbox!"

"I need the blue paint lunchbox!"

The wizard laughed.

"I can't keep handing them out myself."

So the king built a giant warehouse.

Inside the warehouse were thousands of master lunchboxes.

           Warehouse

   📦 Robot Box
   📦 Teddy Box
   📦 Car Box
   📦 Paint Box
   📦 Train Box

Now, whenever a worker needed one,

he simply walked to the warehouse...

picked the correct lunchbox...

and went back to work.

The wizard no longer built the same lunchbox over and over.

The warehouse stored them forever.

The kingdom became much faster.
