# 📖 Chapter 9 — Too Many Workshops

The Story

Business became better than ever.

Soon...

The kingdom had ten workshops.

Then...

one hundred workshops.

Then...

one thousand workshops.

The king was happy...

until the children started placing orders.

Nobody knew where to send them.

Workshop A builds robots.

Workshop B builds teddy bears.

Workshop C builds toy cars.

Workshop D is full.

Workshop E is empty.

Workers began shouting across the kingdom.

"Who builds robots today?"

"Which workshop has room?"

"Where should this order go?"

The lunchboxes worked perfectly.

The workers worked perfectly.

The problem wasn't building toys anymore.

The problem was organizing thousands of workshops.

The king realized...

"We need someone to coordinate everything."

Ending

The next morning...

someone arrived at the palace.

Not a worker.

Not a wizard.

Someone whose only job was organizing the entire kingdom.
